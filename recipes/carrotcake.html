Carrot Cake

Carrot Cake Delight: A Slice of Spiced Sweetness

Embrace the cozy, comforting flavors of our Carrot Cake. This moist and aromatic dessert is a true celebration of sweet, spiced goodness, highlighted by the earthy sweetness of carrots. Savor the perfect blend of flavors and textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Carrots (grated)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven and prepare a cake pan.
  2. Combine grated carrots with flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and sugar until well combined, then mix in vegetable oil and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually fold the dry carrot mixture into the wet ingredients.
  5. If desired, add chopped walnuts or pecans.
  6. Pour the batter into the cake pan and bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the carrot cake to cool before frosting, if desired. Enjoy your Carrot Cake!
