Apple Pie

Classic Apple Pie: A Slice of Homemade Comfort

Indulge in the timeless delight of our Classic Apple Pie. This mouthwatering dessert combines tender, cinnamon-spiced apple slices nestled in a flaky, buttery crust. Savor the warmth and nostalgia of homemade comfort with every bite. Perfect for any occasion, this pie is sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Cornstarch
  • Lemon juice
  • Butter
  • Egg wash

Steps

  1. Prepare the pie crust or use a store-bought one.
  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples.
  3. Toss the apple slices with sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and cornstarch.
  4. Place one crust in a pie dish and add the apple mixture.
  5. Dot the apples with small pieces of butter.
  6. Cover the pie with the second crust and crimp the edges to seal.
  7. Make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  8. Brush the top crust with an egg wash and sprinkle with sugar if desired.
  9. Bake the pie in a preheated oven until it's golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  10. Let the pie cool before serving.
